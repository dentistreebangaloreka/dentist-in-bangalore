Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st September 2017

At Dentistree Advanced Dental and Implant Center, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website https://dentistreebangalore.com/.

We may collect personal information when you:

– Fill out a contact or appointment form

– Interact with our chatbot or WhatsApp link

Information may include:

– Full name
